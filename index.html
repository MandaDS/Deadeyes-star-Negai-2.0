What is Deadeyes-star?

Deadeyes-star.com is a Japanese music database made by the fans, for the fans. Recently we've had time to resurrect our old project and give DS a purpose once more. Now Deadeyes-star is ever growing. We hope to provide up to date and accurate information on the current and past Japanese alternative and indies music scene.
